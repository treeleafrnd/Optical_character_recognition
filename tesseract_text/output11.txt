Filters

+ Will never change between searches.

* Can be selected before and after a search
query has been executed.

+ Most often used in information-rich websites.

+ Low-level classification based on broad
categories.

Facets

+ Will change between searches, depending
on the returned results.

+ Can only be selected after a search query
has been executed.

+ Most often used in ecommerce.

+ Often high-level, specific classification based
on search result attributes.
